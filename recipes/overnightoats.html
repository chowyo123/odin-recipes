Overnight Oats

Description

This hearty, vegetable lasagna is the only lasagna my husband will eat. We love it!!! Hope you all enjoy as much as we do.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • ⅔ cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • ½ small ripe banana
  • 1 tablespoon strawberry preserves

Steps

  1. In wide-mouth pint jar or at least 12-ounce glass, mix oats, chia seeds, and almondmilk; let stand 3 minutes. Stir again.
  2. In small bowl, mash almond butter and banana until almost smooth. Drop over oats mixture. Top with preserves. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Uncover and stir slightly to serve.
